Instructions

How it works: Sudoku is a logical puzzle with missing numbers that needs to be filled into a 9x9 board that consists of rows and unique 3x3 squares. Each square and row consists of numbers from 1 to 9, no number can appear twice in a square or a row.

Your assignment: is to fill all the empty spaces in the board by following the instructions above. To do so, you press the spot you'd like to fill with a number, then that spot will lighten and now you can select the number from the number container on the right.

If the number you chose was correct, the number will turn blue and you can continue.
If the number you chose was incorrect, the number will turn red and one life will be taken away.

Tips: If you get stuck, you sacrifice a life by guessing a number in an empty space. If you play on a computer, press the key F11 for fullscreen.
